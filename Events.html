Key celebrations of this year's Dinagyang Festival

2026 Celebration

A Devotional Return to Basics

The 2026 Dinagyang Festival in Iloilo City featured a deeply devotional theme alongside spectacular street performances. With over 350,000 attendees, the celebration showcased the Ati Tribes Competition, Kasadyahan sa Kabanwahanan, and the ILOmination Street Dance — plus the inaugural Pet Parade.

Ati Tribes Competition

The crown jewel of the festival — warriors in hand-crafted costumes perform at the Iloilo Freedom Grandstand and surrounding stages. This year, Tribu Salognon emerged victorious, electrifying the crowd with their masterful choreography and vibrant presentation.
